## 💾 Local Storage Keys

1. **completedCourses**
   - Array of completed course objects
   - Format: [{ id, title, completedAt }, ...]

2. **quizScores**
   - Array of quiz score objects
   - Format: [{ courseId, score, total, percentage, date }, ...]

3. **theme**
   - String: 'light' or 'dark'
